1. Executive Summary & Market Economics

Standard B2B SDR teams carry an annual fully-loaded cost of $110,000–$154,000 per seat. Traditional cold outreach relies on static contact lists and un-personalized email blasts, yielding low response rates (<2%) and burning domain reputations.

The SignalSDR Solution

SignalSDR automates the outbound lead qualification and outreach process by monitoring real-time intent signals (job changes, funding events, technographic updates, hiring surges). Outreach occurs only when timing is optimal, resulting in:

  • 3x Improvement in Reply Rates (18.4% average across signal-driven campaigns).
  • 60% Lower Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC).
  • Built-in Multi-Jurisdiction Compliance (CAN-SPAM, GDPR, CASL, CCPA).

3. Core Technical Architecture

3.1 Mathematical Signal Scoring Formula

Signals decay over time using an exponential decay model combined with firmographic fit scoring:

Signal Score = Base_Weight * e^(-λ * t)
Composite Score = (Signal Score * 0.6) + (ICP Fit Score * 0.4)

Where λ = 0.05 (Half-life ≈ 14 days), t = days since signal detection
